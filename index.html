Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia, consisting of over 7,000 islands. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, beautiful beaches, and vibrant traditions influenced by Malay, Spanish, American, and Chinese heritage. The Philippine flag features a horizontal bicolor of blue (peace and justice) and red (courage and patriotism). On the left side is a white triangle symbolizing equality and liberty, with a golden sun at its center representing independence. Each of the sun's eight rays stands for a province that led the fight against Spanish rule. In each corner of the triangle is a five-pointed star, representing the country’s three main island groups: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.
